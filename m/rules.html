Don't complain about your or another players punishment. Doing so will extend the punishment on you. This will result in a mute.

Hacking is not allowed. Doing so is a instant ban.

Rule 5

Don't spam. Spamming will result in a 1 hour mute.

Rule 6

Don't ask for OP. Spamming or asking multiple times will result in a mute.

Rule 7

Don't ask for the seed. This counts as cheating. Asking multiple times will result in a mute.

Don't go over 20 animals of each kind. (ex. 20 cows only, 25 sheep only, 20 chickens only, ect.) This is to help reduce lag. Going over the limit will result in you having 48 hours to get rid of some or else your farm will be destroyed.
